About

Storwell is a locally owned, family run company that has been in the self-storage business for over 20 years. The company was founded in 1987 by Gregory D’Atri-Guiran, who continues to run the business to this day with the help of his four adult sons. Storwell’s location on Evans Avenue has been providing high-quality self-storage solutions for Toronto and Etobicoke for decades. Throughout our history, Storwell has maintained the highest standards ensuring that all clients are treated with dignity, respect and made to feel like one of the family.

The Storwell Self Storage Etobicoke facility on Evans Avenue spans 71,000 square feet, has 12 buildings, and nearly 800 units of varying sizes including closet-sized 3 ft x 8 ft units all the way up to industrial-sized 12 ft x 33 ft units that can accommodate any of your storage needs. Conveniently located on the east end of Etobicoke just off Kipling Avenue and the Gardiner Expressway, the facility is just minutes away from CF Sherway Gardens. Whether you are a family moving to a new home, or a student at one of the local colleges, like Humber College’s Lakeshore campus, which is just a few minutes south of Storwell, self-storage can be an extremely useful resource.

Self Storage

Get a Quote
Self-storage is an invaluable resource when you are moving into a new home. One of the keys to a successful move is to pack your items correctly; this is especially true when you are moving long distances.

When moving your belongings from one home to another, it is important that everything is packed properly to avoid any damages during the trip. This is true whether you are hiring a moving company or moving your items yourself. On the way to your new home, boxes will inevitably shift around in the truck. If your boxes are tightly packed with a good amount of protective cushioning, it will help reduce the chances that something gets broken or falls out of the truck during the move.

Obtaining the packing materials necessary for a move can be costly. There are several online retailers that will ship packing supplies to your home, but before you go to these, you should follow up with your local liquor store, bar, or grocery store. These places get their inventory shipped in thick-walled cardboard boxes, which are perfect to use while moving. These stores have a lot of these extra boxes lying around and will be willing to sell you them for exceptionally low prices or even for free. While it is perfectly okay to store items in cardboard boxes inside of your self-storage unit, it is advised to use clear plastic containers that can offer greater protection and make organization easier.
